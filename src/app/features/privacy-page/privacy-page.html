Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Introduction

Flyealo ("we," "us," or "our") operates the travel itinerary platform. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection, use, and protection practices. By using Flyealo, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service. This includes:

Authentication Data

  • Email address: for account creation and communication
  • Username: chosen by you during registration or provided by Google Sign-In
  • Password: encrypted and securely stored (when using email/password authentication)
  • Google Account information: if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your email and name from Google

Travel Preferences

  • Destination cities and regions you select
  • Number of travel days
  • Travel interests and preferences
  • Budget information (if provided)
  • Additional travel details (if provided)

Generated Itineraries

  • Your created itineraries and any modifications you make
  • Saved attractions and customizations

Technical Data

  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and features used (analytics only)
  • Timestamps of account activity

We do NOT collect: Precise location data, payment card information (handled by payment providers), health data, biometric data, or any sensitive personal information beyond what's listed above.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To create, store, and retrieve your personalized itineraries
  • Account Management: To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • AI Personalization: To generate itineraries tailored to your preferences
  • Communication: To send important account updates, service changes, and support responses
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve platform features (anonymized data only)
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We use your data based on our legitimate interest in providing a secure, reliable service and your consent to our privacy policy.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

Current Policy (February 2025): We do NOT currently sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Limited Third-Party Access

We may share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that assist us in operating the platform (hosting, analytics, authentication)
  • Google: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email and name (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Payment Processors: Payment information is handled by third-party processors (we never see full card details)
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law or court order

Future Data Sharing

If we ever change this policy to share data with third parties, we will:

  • Notify you at least 30 days in advance via email
  • Provide an opt-out option
  • Give you time to delete your account if you disagree

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on secure servers in Europe and processed in compliance with GDPR and other data protection regulations.

Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption of passwords using industry-standard algorithms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data (employees only when necessary)
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems

While we implement strong security measures, no system is completely secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately at hello@flyealo.com.

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal retention requirements). This is often called "Right to be Forgotten."

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a portable format to transfer to another service.

Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data while you verify its accuracy.

Right to Object

You can object to certain processing activities, including direct marketing.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, send a request to hello@flyealo.com with:

  • Your full name and email address
  • A clear description of your request
  • Any relevant account details

We will respond within 30 days (or as required by law).

Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active.

When you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days, except:

  • Data required for legal/tax compliance (retained for 7 years)
  • Aggregated/anonymized data used for analytics
  • Data necessary to prevent fraud or abuse

Backups may retain your data for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes.

Cookies & Analytics

Flyealo uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

  • Keeping you logged in
  • Remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Analyzing how you use the platform (Google Analytics)
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use Flyealo.

Children's Privacy

Flyealo is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

Flyealo is primarily designed for European users and complies with GDPR. If you are outside the EU, your data may be transferred to European servers. By using Flyealo, you consent to this transfer.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at:

Email: hello@flyealo.com

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

Policy Updates

Flyealo reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Material changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on the platform. Your continued use of Flyealo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
